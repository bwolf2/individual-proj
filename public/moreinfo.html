Company History

100 years ago, our heroic founder, Jack "The Wack" Waver, founded Wavyman Company because he had the revolutionary vision to one day allow anyone who loved the comaraderie of the wave to be able to share that love in a colorful, exciting, and obnoxious way. Today, Wavyman Company Inc. serves as Jack's lasting impact on the world, reminding us that it's never a bad time to get wavy.
